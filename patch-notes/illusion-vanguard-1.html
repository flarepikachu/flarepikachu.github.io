Illusion Vanguard Hotfix - v1.0.1
Wave Defense
General
  • All players now respawn after completing a wave.
  • Players now die if they leave the game.
  • Added 3 new mobs.
Endless Mode
  • Bosses now spawn every 10 waves instead of every 20.
  • Reduced insignia gain from mobs by 50%.
  • Mobs will now scale into higher tiers faster.
Class Balance

Spiritguard
  • Reduced Spiritguard's ally cooldown reduction from 1s to 0.5s.
  • Spiritguard can only have two instances of soulbinding active at once.
Apothecary
  • Reduced Apothecary's Leech upgrade from 2.5/5/7.5/10% to 1.25/2.5/3.75/5%.
Mob Balance
  • Increased Mithra's Soul Switch cooldown from 3s to 4s.
  • Reduced Illusion Swordsman's energy drain from 15 to 10.
Guilds
  • Changed win requirment to make a guild from 20 to 10.
Bug Fixes
  • Changed BOUND message color to gray instead of aqua.
  • Fixed playercount in spectator menu.
  • Fixed health powerups showing decimals.
  • Fixed scoreboard sometimes flickering when another game ends.
  • Fixed Soul Switch not going on cooldown in PvE.
  • Fixed skeletons causing the server to explode on rare occasions.
