Crusader

A defense-oriented Paladin specialization that inspires allies by granting them more energy in battle and has access to minor healing.

Crusader's Strike

Energy Cost: 90

Crit Chance: 25%

Crit Multiplier: 175%

Strike the targeted enemy, dealing 326-441 damage and restoring 24 energy to 2 nearby allies within 10 blocks.

Marked allies get priority in restoring energy. Every time marked allies gain energy, they will also get a 40% Speed increase for 1 second.

Consecrate

Cooldown: 7.8s

Energy Cost: 50

Crit Chance: 15%

Crit Multiplier: 200%

Consecrate the ground below your feet, declaring it sacred. Enemies standing on it will take 144 - 194.4 damage per second and take 15% increased damage from your paladin strikes. Lasts 5 seconds.

Light Infusion

Cooldown: 15.7s

You become infused with light, restoring 120 energy and increasing your movement speed by 40% for 3 seconds.

Holy Radiance

Cooldown: 19.6s

Energy Cost: 20

Crit Chance: 15%

Crit Multiplier: 175%

Radiate with holy energy, healing yourself and all nearby allies for 582 - 760 health.

You may look at an ally to mark them for 8 seconds. Crusader's Mark increases the allied player's energy regeneration by 6 and their speed by 25%.

Crusader's Mark has an optimal range of 15 blocks.

Inspiring Presence

Cooldown: 70.5s

Your presence on the battlefield inspires your allies, increasing their energy regeneration by 10 per second and their movement speed by 30%. Lasts 12 seconds.
