Benevolent

Under the benevolent rule of Physira and Mithra, the kingdom prospered.

Benevolent is a Legendary weapon title that can be unlocked for 50,000 coins and 1,000 shards, with no additional requirements.

Weapon Stats

Melee Damage: 120-140

Crit Chance: 20%

Crit Multiplier: 175%

Health Bonus: 800

Speed Bonus: 10%

Skill Crit Chance: 5%

Skill Crit Multiplier: 5%

Passive Ability

Increase all healing done by equipped player by 25%.

Trivia
  • Title name and ability concept suggested by Flare.
