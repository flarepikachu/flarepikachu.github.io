Requiem

The wisps of illusion propel the requiem of the kingdom.

Requiem is a Legendary weapon title that can be unlocked for 50,000 coins and 1,000 shards, with no additional requirements.

Weapon Stats

Melee Damage: 120-140

Crit Chance: 20%

Crit Multiplier: 175%

Health Bonus: 800

Speed Bonus: 10%

Skill Crit Chance: 2%

Skill Crit Multiplier: 4%

Passive Ability

Increase Healing and Damage by 20% for 60s for each dead ally.
