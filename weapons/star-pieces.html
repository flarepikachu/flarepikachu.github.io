Star Pieces

Star Pieces are obtained from the Masterworks Fair, and if youâ€™re really lucky, Susan. Star Pieces can be applied at the Weaponsmith.

  • Only Legendary Weapons can have Star Pieces applied on them.
  • Star Pieces provide a 20%/30%/40%/50% stat boost to a random stat upon application, based on the rarity of the Star Piece (Common/Rare/Epic/Legendary).
  • Once a Star Piece is applied on a weapon, the Star Piece is consumed.
  • Star Pieces can be applied to a weapon multiple times. The newer Star Piece will overwrite the previous Star Piece bonus. Only one Star Piece bonus can be applied at a time.
  • When changing the title of a weapon, the Star Piece bonus will be removed from the weapon. The Star Piece will not be refunded.
  • Star Pieces cannot target a negative stat.
