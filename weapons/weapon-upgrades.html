Weapon Upgrades

Weapon Upgrades are a similar concept to Void Forging in Hypixel Warlords. Weapons can be upgraded at the Weaponsmith.

  • Only Epic and Legendary weapons are eligible for being upgraded.
  • Each weapon upgrade will increase each weapon stat by 10%.
  • The 10% upgrade is based upon the previous weapon’s stats and not the weapon’s base stats.
  • Negative weapon stats will be decreased by 10%.
  • Epic weapons can be upgraded up to 2 times while Legendary weapons can be upgraded up to 4 times.

Weapon upgrade costs are as below:
